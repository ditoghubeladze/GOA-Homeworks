Museum

1. Mona Lisa (Left) – Painted by Leonardo da Vinci in the early 16th century, this portrait of a woman with a mysterious smile is one of the most iconic artworks in the world. It is housed in the Louvre Museum in Paris.

2. The Starry Night (Middle) – Created by Vincent van Gogh in 1889, this painting depicts a swirling night sky over a quiet village. It is one of Van Gogh's most recognized works and is displayed at the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) in New York.

3. The Scream (Right) – Painted by Edvard Munch in 1893, this expressionist artwork captures a figure with a distorted face screaming in anguish against a dramatic sky. It symbolizes existential anxiety and is housed in the National Gallery of Norway.
